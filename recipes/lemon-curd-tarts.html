Lemon Curd Tarts

Description

These delightful lemon curd tarts are a go-to dessert for tea parties, and they’re quick to prepare without the need for precise measurements. Using a pastry bag to fill the small phyllo shells, you can make them in advance— the crust stays wonderfully crunchy for hours. Perfect for tea parties, as well as weddings and baby showers, these tarts are always a hit!

Recipe submitted by Paula, Allrecipes

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup lemon curd
  • 1 (1.9 ounce) container frozen miniature phyllo shells, thawed
  • ½ cup frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon, or to taste

Steps

  1. Spoon 1 teaspoon lemon curd into the bottom of each phyllo shell; top with whipped topping. Sprinkle a light dusting of cinnamon over the top.

Variations

  1. Place 1/4 teaspoon raspberry jam underneath lemon curd and garnish with a fresh raspberry, omitting the cinnamon.
  2. Use lime curd instead of lemon, both of which can be found in the baking aisle of the grocery store.
